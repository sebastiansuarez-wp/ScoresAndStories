## Working title (factual)
Five real deadlines for NYC students, June through October 2026

## The deadlines, soonest first

**June 15 (priority) and June 29 (final): Schomburg Junior Scholars Program, NYPL. [Both]**
* Tuition free Saturday scholars program at the Schomburg Center, Harlem, academic year 2026 to 27.
* 100 students per year, grades 6 through 12. Lectures, collections research, Annual Youth Summit in May.
* Source: https://www.nypl.org/about/fellowships-institutes/schomburg-junior-scholars-program (verified June 10).
* Grade group: 6 to 12. Wagner angle: a yearlong named credential at a world famous research center, applied for in one week.

**August 23: Student Historian Internship, New York Historical. [Both]**
* Paid ($1,200 stipend), yearlong, Wednesday evenings. Entering grades 10 to 12, NYC metro including parts of NJ and CT.
* Source: https://www.nyhistory.org/education/student-historian-internship-program (verified June 10).
* Grade group: entering 10 to 12. Wagner angle: the strongest paid humanities credential with a summer application window.

**October 26, 12 PM ET: Congressional App Challenge. [Scores]**
* Free district by district coding competition, middle and high school, teams up to four. Open now; the summer is the build window.
* Source: https://www.congressionalappchallenge.us/ (verified June 10).
* Grade group: 6 to 12. Wagner angle: a congressional award for a project built between now and October.

**Expected September: The Met High School Internship posting. [Both]**
* $1,100 stipend, grades 10 and 11, school year cycle. Pattern: posting September, deadline late October. The recommendation letter is the long lead item.
* Source: https://www.metmuseum.org/about-the-met/internships/high-school (pattern, reconfirm at posting).
* Grade group: 10 and 11. Wagner angle: the prep happens in June (ask the teacher), not at the deadline.

**January 2027 pattern: Biorocket, Genspace. [Both]**
* $2,000 stipend, genetic engineering research, free. At least 16 years old, NYC public or charter school within a 45 minute commute to Sunset Park.
* Source: https://www.genspace.org/biorocket (program verified June 10; exact 2027 deadline posts later).
* Grade group: current 10th and 11th. Wagner angle: the January deadlines are decided by who knew in June.

## Flagged, not recommended
* June 12: NYU SPS Career Edge commuter application. $2,579 per one week course plus $50 fee, open admission. Pay to play pattern flagged; every free item above carries more selective weight.

* June is recommendation letter season for September postings (Met). Build that into family check ins now.
* The summer build window (App Challenge) suits Builders who resist application essays: the artifact is the application.
